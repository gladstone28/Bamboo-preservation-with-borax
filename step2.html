Mixing the Solution

Prepare the borate solution by dissolving borate powder in warm water. A commonly recommended mixture involves a 1:1:10 ratio of boric acid, borax, and water by weight. This creates a 10% solution that is effective for bamboo preservation. Ensure the solution is thoroughly mixed until all the powders are completely dissolved.

Safety Measures

When handling borate powder and mixing the solution, wear protective gloves and eyewear to avoid skin and eye irritation. Work in a well-ventilated area to minimize inhalation of any dust or fumes.
