Application Methods

To ensure the bamboo is well-preserved, the borate solution needs to be thoroughly applied. There are several methods to do this:

  • Soaking: Submerge the bamboo in the borate solution for at least 72 hours. This method ensures deep penetration of the solution into the bamboo fibers.
  • Brushing or Spraying: If soaking is not feasible, you can also apply the solution using a brush or spray. This may require multiple applications to ensure adequate coverage and absorption.
  • Pressure Treating: For commercial operations, pressure treating the bamboo with the solution ensures deep penetration and uniform treatment.

Ensuring Effective Treatment

After applying the solution, allow the bamboo to rest for a few hours to ensure the solution is absorbed properly. For soaking, ensure that the bamboo is fully submerged and consider using weights to keep it under the solution.
